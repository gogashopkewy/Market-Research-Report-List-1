<p><p>EMI shielding paste is categorized based on its volume resistivity, which measures a material's ability to resist electrical conductivity. The first type, with a volume resistivity under 2.5 x 10^-5 Ω·cm, offers excellent conductivity, suitable for high-frequency applications needing robust shielding. The second type, with a volume resistivity ranging from 4.0 x 10^-5 to 9 x 10^-5 Ω·cm, balances conductivity and insulation, making it ideal for moderate shielding applications in electronics where some electrical insulation is also required.</p></p>
