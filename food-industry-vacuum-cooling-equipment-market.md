<p><p>The food industry vacuum cooling equipment market offers two primary types: vertical and horizontal vacuum cooling equipment. Vertical vacuum cooling equipment utilizes a tower-like structure, allowing steam to rise and be condensed efficiently, suitable for bulk products. In contrast, horizontal vacuum cooling equipment features a flat design, facilitating the cooling of smaller or more delicate items with a uniform heat transfer. Both types enhance food preservation by rapidly reducing temperature, thus extending shelf life and maintaining product quality.</p></p>
