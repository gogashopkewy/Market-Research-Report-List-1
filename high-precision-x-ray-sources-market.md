<p><ul><li>Open-tube</li><li>Sealed-tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-precision X-ray sources market is primarily divided into two categories: open-tube and sealed-tube systems. Open-tube X-ray sources utilize a vacuum tube where electrons are emitted from a cathode and directed towards a target, allowing for adjustable parameters and high output, but requiring regular maintenance. In contrast, sealed-tube X-ray sources contain the vacuum within a durable, closed housing, offering enhanced stability, longevity, and reduced maintenance needs, making them ideal for industrial applications and environments requiring consistent performance.</p></p>
