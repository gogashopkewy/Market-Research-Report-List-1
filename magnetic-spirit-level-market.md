<p><p>The magnetic spirit level market includes two primary types: standard spirit levels and digital spirit levels. Standard spirit levels utilize a bubble tube to indicate horizontality or verticality, often equipped with magnetic strips for easy attachment to metal surfaces. In contrast, digital spirit levels feature electronic displays that provide precise angle measurements, often with additional features like data logging and sound alerts. Both types cater to various construction and DIY applications, enhancing accuracy and efficiency in leveling tasks.</p></p>
