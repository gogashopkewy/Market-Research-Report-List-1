<p><p>Diabetic retinopathy treatment options include several key market types. Laser surgery focuses on sealing leaking blood vessels and reducing vision loss. Injection of corticosteroids helps reduce inflammation and fluid leakage in the retina. Anti-VEGF drugs are used to inhibit vascular growth factors that lead to abnormal blood vessel formation and further retinal damage. Vitrectomy involves the surgical removal of the vitreous gel to address severe retinal damage or bleeding, enhancing the overall management of the condition.</p></p>
