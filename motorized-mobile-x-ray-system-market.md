<p><p>The Motorized Mobile X-ray System market can be categorized primarily into two types: Digital Radiography and Analog Radiography. Digital Radiography employs electronic detectors to convert X-ray signals into digital images, offering enhanced image quality, quick processing times, and advanced imaging features. In contrast, Analog Radiography relies on traditional film-based methods to capture images, typically requiring manual processing. While digital systems are increasingly preferred for their efficiency and precision, analog systems are still in use in certain healthcare settings due to cost considerations.</p></p>
