<p><ul><li>XLPE Insulated Cable</li><li>Mineral Insulated Cable</li><li>Alloy Mineral Insulated Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The insulated cable and wire market comprises various types, including XLPE insulated cables, mineral insulated cables, and alloy mineral insulated cables. XLPE insulated cables use cross-linked polyethylene for enhanced thermal and mechanical properties, making them ideal for power transmission. Mineral insulated cables feature a copper or aluminum core surrounded by mineral insulation, offering superior fire resistance and performance in extreme conditions. Alloy mineral insulated cables combine metal conductors with specialized insulation, providing lightweight, flexible solutions for demanding environments like industrial and marine applications.</p></p>
