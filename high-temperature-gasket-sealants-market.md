<p><p>The high temperature gasket sealants market primarily consists of two types: paste and liquid. Paste sealants are thick, providing robust adhesion and resistance to thermal expansion, making them ideal for irregular surfaces and high-stress applications. Liquid sealants, on the other hand, offer easier application, including the ability to fill gaps and crevices effectively. They cure to form a strong bond that withstands elevated temperatures, making both types suitable for automotive, aerospace, and industrial applications where thermal resilience is critical.</p></p>
