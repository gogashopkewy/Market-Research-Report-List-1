<p><p>Unidirectional (UD) tapes are composite materials consisting of fibers aligned in a single direction, enhancing strength and stiffness. The market for UD tapes is classified into thermoplastic and thermoset types. </p><p>Thermoplastic UD tapes are processed at high temperatures and can be re-formed, offering advantages in weight and scalability. In contrast, thermoset UD tapes undergo a curing process that provides superior thermal and chemical resistance, making them ideal for demanding applications in aerospace, automotive, and construction industries.</p></p>
