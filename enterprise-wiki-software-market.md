<p><p>Enterprise wiki software is categorized into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions are installed and run on a company's own servers, offering greater control, customization, and data security but requiring more maintenance and upfront investment. In contrast, cloud-based solutions are hosted by providers on their servers, allowing for easier scalability, automatic updates, and reduced IT overhead. This model promotes accessibility and collaboration across distributed teams, making it increasingly popular among organizations.</p></p>
