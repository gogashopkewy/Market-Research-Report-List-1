<p><strong>The Ultra Wide Field Retinal Imaging Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OCT Camera</li><li>Fundus Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra Wide Field Retinal Imaging Device market encompasses advanced imaging technologies primarily including OCT (Optical Coherence Tomography) cameras and fundus cameras. OCT cameras provide high-resolution cross-sectional images of the retina, enabling detailed examination of retinal layers and structures, essential for diagnosing various eye conditions. In contrast, fundus cameras capture wide-field color images of the retina, facilitating the identification of conditions such as diabetic retinopathy and glaucoma. Both technologies enhance clinical diagnostics and treatment planning in ophthalmology.</p></p>
