<p><p>The plastic injection mold market comprises several types, including multi-cavity molds, mono-cavity molds, and others. Multi-cavity molds are designed to produce multiple identical parts in a single cycle, enhancing production efficiency. Mono-cavity molds focus on producing one part per cycle, ideal for specialized or low-volume production. Other molds encompass a variety of designs, including family molds and stack molds, catering to specific product requirements. Each type serves distinct manufacturing needs, impacting efficiency, cost, and output quality.</p></p>
