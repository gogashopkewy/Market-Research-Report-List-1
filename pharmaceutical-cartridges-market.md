<p><p>The pharmaceutical cartridges market primarily comprises two types: glass and plastic cartridges. Glass cartridges are favored for their chemical resistance and ability to maintain sterility, making them suitable for sensitive medications. They are often used for parenteral drugs and have a high barrier performance. In contrast, plastic cartridges are lightweight and shatterproof, offering flexibility in design and manufacturing. They are increasingly popular for their cost-effectiveness and ease of use in various applications, including insulin delivery and other biologics.</p></p>
