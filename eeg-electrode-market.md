<p><p>The EEG electrode market is primarily divided into two types: repeatable and disposable electrodes. In contrast, disposable EEG electrodes are intended for single-use, ensuring high hygiene standards and minimizing cross-contamination risks. These electrodes are often preferred in settings requiring quick setups, such as emergency rooms or outpatient procedures, where efficiency and safety are paramount.</p></p>
