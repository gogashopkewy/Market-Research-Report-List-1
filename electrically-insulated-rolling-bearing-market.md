<p><ul><li>"Coated Bearing"</li><li>"Hybrid Bearing"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrically insulated rolling bearing market features two primary types: coated bearings and hybrid bearings. Coated bearings utilize a non-conductive coating to prevent electrical current passage, reducing wear and extending life in electrical applications. Hybrid bearings, on the other hand, combine ceramic rolling elements with steel races, offering electrical insulation alongside reduced friction and enhanced durability. Both types are designed to protect machinery from electrical damage, making them essential in industries where electrical issues are a concern.</p></p>
