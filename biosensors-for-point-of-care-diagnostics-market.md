<p><ul><li>Electrochemical Biosensors</li><li>Thermal Biosensors</li><li>Piezoelectric Biosensors</li><li>Accelerometer Biosensors</li><li>Optical Biosensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The point-of-care diagnostics market for biosensors includes several key types. Electrochemical biosensors utilize electrode-based detection methods for rapid analysis. Thermal biosensors measure heat changes to identify specific biomolecules. Piezoelectric biosensors detect mass changes via frequency shifts, offering high sensitivity. Accelerometer biosensors monitor motion-related parameters, often integrated with wearable devices. Optical biosensors rely on light properties to detect analytes, providing non-invasive and precise measurements. Each type contributes unique advantages to enhance medical testing and monitoring.</p></p>
