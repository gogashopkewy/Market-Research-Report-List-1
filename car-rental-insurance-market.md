<p><p>The car rental insurance market consists of several key types. Insurance for vehicle loss covers damages to the rental car itself due to accidents, theft, or vandalism. Third-party insurance offers protection against claims from other drivers for injury or damage caused while using the rental vehicle. Pilfer insurance addresses losses incurred from theft of personal belongings left inside the vehicle. Other market options can include liability coverage, roadside assistance, and personal accident coverage, catering to a variety of customer needs.</p></p>
