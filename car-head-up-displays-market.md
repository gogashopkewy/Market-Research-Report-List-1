<p><ul><li>Windshield Head-Up Display</li><li>Integrated Head-Up Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car head-up display (HUD) market primarily features two types: Windshield Head-Up Displays and Integrated Head-Up Displays. Windshield HUDs project critical information directly onto the vehicle's windshield, enhancing visibility and reducing driver distraction. These systems utilize advanced optics to display speed, navigation, and alerts seamlessly within the driver’s line of sight. Integrated HUDs, on the other hand, are built into the dashboard or other components, offering a sleek design while providing essential data without obstructing the driver’s view. Both types improve driving safety and convenience.</p></p>
