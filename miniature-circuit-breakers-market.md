<p><p>The Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCBs) market encompasses three main types: B Type MCBs, which are designed for domestic and light commercial applications, offering protection against overloads and short circuits for devices with normal inrush currents; C Type MCBs, suitable for commercial and industrial environments, allowing higher inrush currents for equipment like motors; and D Type MCBs, which are used for applications with very high inrush currents, such as transformers and generators, providing robust protection in demanding conditions.</p></p>
