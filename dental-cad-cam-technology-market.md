Milling machines are used for precisely fabricating dental restorations from blocks of material. Furnaces are essential for the heat processing of ceramics and metals, ensuring durability and aesthetics in dental products. Integrated systems combine both software and hardware solutions, enabling seamless design, fabrication, and finishing processes, thus enhancing efficiency and accuracy in dental laboratory workflows. Together, these technologies streamline dental restoration production.</p></p>
