<p><ul><li>Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzer</li><li>PEM Electrolyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The benchtop hydrogen gas generators market primarily includes two main types: traditional alkaline electrolyzers and PEM (Proton Exchange Membrane) electrolyzers. Traditional alkaline electrolyzers use an alkaline solution and are known for their cost-effectiveness and reliability, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, PEM electrolyzers utilize a solid membrane and offer higher efficiency, faster response times, and compact designs, which are beneficial for dynamic hydrogen production needs. Both technologies cater to diverse industrial and research applications.</p></p>
