<p><p>Automotive Tire-Pressure Monitoring Systems (TPMS) enhance vehicle safety by monitoring tire pressure in real-time. There are two primary types: Direct TPMS and Indirect TPMS. Direct TPMS uses dedicated sensors in each tire to provide precise pressure readings, alerting drivers to any deviations. In contrast, Indirect TPMS utilizes the vehicle's anti-lock braking system sensors to estimate tire pressure based on wheel rotation speed. Both systems play a crucial role in preventing tire-related accidents and improving fuel efficiency.</p></p>
