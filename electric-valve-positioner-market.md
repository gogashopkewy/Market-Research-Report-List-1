<p><strong>The Electric Valve Positioner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Acting Positioner</li><li>Double Acting Positioner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric valve positioner market is categorized into single acting and double acting positioners. Single acting positioners control valves that rely on a spring to return to their default position when not actuated, making them simpler and often more cost-effective. In contrast, double acting positioners utilize air pressure to both open and close the valve, offering more precise and faster control. Both types are essential in various industrial processes, providing accurate valve positioning for optimal system performance.</p></p>
