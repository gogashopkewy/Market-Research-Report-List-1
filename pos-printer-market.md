<p><strong>The POS Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impact type</li><li>Thermal type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The POS printer market can be categorized mainly into impact and thermal types. Impact printers use a mechanism that physically strikes an inked ribbon against the paper, suitable for multi-part forms, but they are generally slower and noisier. Conversely, thermal printers employ heat to print on specially coated paper, offering faster, quieter, and more cost-effective solutions, especially for receipts and labels. The choice between the two depends on application needs, print volume, and cost considerations.</p></p>
