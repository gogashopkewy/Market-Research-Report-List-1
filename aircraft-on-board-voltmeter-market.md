AC Type voltmeters, on the other hand, measure alternating current voltage, essential for analyzing power distribution in electrical systems. Each type serves critical functions in aircraft operations, ensuring the reliable monitoring and management of onboard electrical systems for safety and performance efficiency.</p></p>
