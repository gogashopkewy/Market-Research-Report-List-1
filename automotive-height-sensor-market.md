<p><p>The automotive height sensor market comprises two main types: analog and digital sensors. Analog height sensors provide continuous voltage output proportional to the vehicle's height, offering simplicity and reliability. In contrast, digital height sensors deliver precise measurements through digital signals, enhancing accuracy and integration with advanced electronic control systems. Both types are crucial for applications like air suspension systems, contributing to vehicle stability, comfort, and safety by accurately detecting and adjusting ride heights based on real-time conditions.</p></p>
