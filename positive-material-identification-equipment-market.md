<p><p>Positive Material Identification (PMI) equipment is crucial for ensuring material quality and composition in various industries. The two primary types are X-Ray Fluorescence (XRF) and Optical Emission Spectrometry (OES). XRF utilizes X-ray technology to analyze materials quickly and non-destructively, making it ideal for field applications. In contrast, OES involves exciting atoms with a spark and measuring emitted light to determine elemental composition, providing high accuracy for metal alloys. Both methods play vital roles in quality assurance and compliance.</p></p>
