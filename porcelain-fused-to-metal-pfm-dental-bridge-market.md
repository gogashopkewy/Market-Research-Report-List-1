<p><p>The Porcelain Fused to Metal (PFM) dental bridge market encompasses various types catering to different patient needs. Traditional bridges involve anchoring to adjacent teeth, offering stability. Cantilever bridges extend from one side only, ideal for limited space. Maryland bridges feature a resin-bonded mechanism, minimizing tooth reduction. Implant-supported bridges rely on dental implants for anchorage, providing enhanced support and longevity. Each type addresses specific clinical situations, patient preferences, and aesthetic requirements, ensuring comprehensive options in restorative dentistry.</p></p>
