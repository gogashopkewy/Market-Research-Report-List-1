<p><p>Electric Thermal Energy Storage (ETES) technology involves the storage of thermal energy produced during off-peak hours, which can then be utilized during peak demand periods. This technology is gaining traction as it provides a cost-effective solution for balancing energy loads, improving grid reliability, and integrating renewable energy sources like solar and wind.
