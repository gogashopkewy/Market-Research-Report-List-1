<p><p>The polypropylene raffia yarn market consists of three primary types: flat, twisted, and spooled. Flat yarn is characterized by its wide, tape-like structure, commonly used in applications requiring a broad surface area. Twisted yarn features a spiral formation, enhancing strength and durability, making it suitable for woven products. Spooled yarn is wound onto spools for convenient handling and storage, often used in industrial applications where ready-to-use material is essential. Each type serves distinct functions in various industries, including packaging and textiles.</p></p>
