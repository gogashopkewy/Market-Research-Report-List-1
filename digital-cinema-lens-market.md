<p><ul><li>Prime Lens</li><li>Zoom Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital cinema lens market primarily consists of two types: prime lenses and zoom lenses. Prime lenses feature a fixed focal length, offering superior optical quality and wider apertures, making them ideal for low-light conditions and artistic effects. In contrast, zoom lenses provide variable focal lengths, offering versatility and convenience for filmmakers who require adaptability without changing lenses frequently. Both types are essential for different shooting scenarios, catering to varying creative needs in digital filmmaking and production environments.</p></p>
