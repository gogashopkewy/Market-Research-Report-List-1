<p><p>The wafer frame market includes various types designed for different applications in semiconductor manufacturing. Dicing wafer frames are used for holding wafers during cutting processes, ensuring precision and stability. UV film wafer frames support the application of photosensitive films in photolithography. Fixed wafer frames provide a sturdy, non-adjustable base for wafer handling, while stick wafer frames use adhesive to hold wafers in place, allowing for easy manipulation. Each type caters to specific needs in the wafer processing workflow, enhancing efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
