<p><p>The foliar fertilizers market is categorized into four primary types: </p><p>1. **Nitrogenous Fertilizers** enhance plant growth and chlorophyll production, boosting yields.</p><p>2. **Phosphatic Fertilizers** support root development and energy transfer within plants, essential for flowering and fruiting.</p><p>3. **Potassic Fertilizers** improve disease resistance and enhance fruit quality through better water regulation and photosynthesis.</p><p>4. **Micronutrient Fertilizers** supply essential trace elements, critical for various enzymatic functions, promoting overall plant health and productivity. Each type addresses specific nutrient needs for optimized plant growth.</p></p>
