<p><p>The superalloy market is primarily categorized into three types: nickel-based, iron-based, and cobalt-based superalloys. Nickel-based superalloys are known for their high-temperature strength and corrosion resistance, making them ideal for aerospace and gas turbine applications. Iron-based superalloys offer a balance of performance and cost, often used in automotive and industrial sectors. Cobalt-based superalloys exhibit superior wear resistance and stability at high temperatures, commonly utilized in turbine blades and dental applications. Each type serves specific industrial needs based on performance requirements.</p></p>
