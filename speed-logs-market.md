<p><p>The market for speed logs encompasses various technologies used to measure vessel speed through water. Electromagnetic sensors utilize electromagnetic fields to determine speed, while Doppler speed logs measure frequency shifts in reflected waves. Pitometer systems calculate speed via pressure differences, and impeller logs utilize rotating blades to gauge water flow. GPS-based speed logs leverage satellite systems for precise positioning and speed detection. Each type caters to specific maritime needs, contributing to a diverse market driven by technological advancements and maritime safety regulations.</p></p>
