<p><strong>The Polycarbonate Panel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Wall Panels</li><li>Corrugated Panels</li><li>Solid Panels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polycarbonate panel market encompasses three main types: multi-wall panels, corrugated panels, and solid panels. Multi-wall panels feature multiple layers, providing enhanced insulation and durability, making them ideal for roofing and greenhouses. Corrugated panels, with their ribbed structure, offer excellent strength-to-weight ratios and are commonly used in commercial buildings and industrial applications. Solid panels, being thicker and more rigid, are favored for applications requiring high impact resistance and optical clarity, such as in skylights or protective barriers.</p></p>
